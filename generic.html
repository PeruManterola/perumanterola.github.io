Rolando The Majestic

Help Rolando, a young white glove thief that got everything taken from him avenge the murder of his parents by the hands of the italian mafia. Hide in plain sight, scour the environment for clues and use supernatural powers to avenge your parents in this stealth based narrative adventure.

This game was made as a final project in my last year of University together with other students. It was released in Steam in June 2022.

My main role on the development was a programer, developing several key systems for the game such as the quest system, inventory system, dialogue system and implementation with Fmod for managing sounds in game. I also worked on level-design and setup in engine from blockout to final product.
